Medical and regulatory disclaimer: Glu AI is not medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or a substitute for professional healthcare. Glu AI is not a medical device (including software-as-a-medical-device), not a continuous glucose monitoring (CGM) system or replacement for one, not a generic calorie tracker aimed at treating disease, and it does not prescribe, guarantee, or personalize medical outcomes. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional regarding glucose management, medications, insulin, allergies, metabolic conditions, and any dietary or lifestyle changes.

What Glu AI is: an educational meal-awareness companion that offers general educational information—based on broadly available knowledge about foods—about whether certain foods might be more or less associated with noticeable glucose responses for some people.

Glu AI does not diagnose conditions, prescribe or adjust therapies, interpret your personal glucose readings, or replace clinician judgment. Outputs are informational and educational only.
