PushX Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

The short version: PushX does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All workout data stays on your device. We have no servers, no analytics, and no tracking.

What data does PushX access?

PushX uses your iPhone's front camera to detect your body position during pushup exercises. This is the only device capability the app accesses.

Is my camera footage recorded or stored?

No. Camera frames are processed in real-time in memory and immediately discarded. No video, images, or camera data are ever written to disk, stored, or transmitted anywhere.

What data is stored on my device?

PushX stores your workout history locally on your iPhone using Apple's SwiftData framework. This includes:

  • Session timestamps (when you started and stopped)
  • Rep count per session
  • Form scores (depth, alignment, consistency)
  • Per-rep measurements (duration, depth, shoulder asymmetry)
  • Improvement suggestions

This data never leaves your device. It is not synced to any cloud service, backend, or third-party platform.

Does PushX collect analytics or usage data?

No. PushX contains no analytics SDKs, no crash reporting services, no event tracking, and no telemetry of any kind. We literally cannot see how you use the app.

Does PushX use third-party services?

No. PushX does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that collect data. The AI pose detection runs entirely on-device using MediaPipe and Apple Vision — no cloud processing.

Does PushX require an account?

No. There is no sign-up, no login, no account creation. You download the app and start using it immediately.

Does PushX share data with anyone?

No. We do not share, sell, or transfer any data to any third party. There is no data to share — we don't collect any.

Can I export my data?

Yes. PushX includes a "Copy for AI Coach" feature that lets you copy your workout data as structured JSON to your clipboard. This is entirely user-initiated — the data goes to your clipboard and nowhere else.

Can I delete my data?

Yes. You can delete individual workout sessions from the History screen (swipe left on any session). Deleting the app removes all stored data permanently.

Children's Privacy

PushX does not collect any data from anyone, including children. The app is rated 4+ and is safe for all ages.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change this policy (for example, if we add optional cloud features in the future), we will update this page and note the date of the change. Any data collection would always be opt-in and clearly disclosed.
