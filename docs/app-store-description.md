## Description

PushX watches your pushups in real time and tells you how to do them better.

Place your phone on the floor, get into position, and start. PushX uses advanced on-device AI to detect your body, count every rep automatically, and score your form — depth, alignment, and consistency — all without a trainer.

WHAT PUSHX DOES:
- Counts your pushup reps in real time using your front camera
- Scores your form after every set (depth, shoulder alignment, rep consistency)
- Shows exactly where your form breaks down and how to fix it
- Tracks your improvement over time with a visual trend chart
- Stores your full workout history on-device

HOW IT WORKS:
1. Lean your phone against a wall at floor level, screen facing you
2. Get into pushup position about 2-3 feet away
3. Tap Start — PushX locks onto your body and starts counting
4. When you're done, tap Stop and see your form breakdown

PRIVACY FIRST:
- All processing happens on your phone. No video is recorded. No data leaves your device. Ever.
- No account required. No sign-up. No tracking.

BUILT FOR PEOPLE WHO TRAIN ALONE:
PushX is for anyone doing pushups at home, in a hotel room, or at the park — anyone who wants honest feedback on their form without paying for a trainer.

Your form score and improvement suggestions are exportable as structured data you can share with a coach or AI assistant for deeper analysis.
